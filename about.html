About Us

AI Receptionist was created to help local businesses stop losing customers because of missed calls, busy front desks, and after-hours inquiries. Our mission is simple: Make every business available 24/7 without hiring more staff.

With advanced AI call handling, appointment booking, emergency routing, and lead qualification, we help businesses automate the front desk so they can focus on providing excellent service to customers.

Today, we support businesses across multiple industries: medical, dental, real estate, restaurants, home services, dealerships, and many more.

Whether you're a solo entrepreneur or a large organization, we help you stay connected to your customers — day and night.
