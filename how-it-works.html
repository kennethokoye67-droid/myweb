How The AI Receptionist Works

The AI answers instantly — 24/7 — with a friendly human-like voice, customized to your brand.

2. The AI Understands What They Need

It handles questions, appointment bookings, lead qualification, emergencies, and general inquiries.

Integrated with Google Calendar, iCloud, Outlook, Calendly, TidyCal, and more. No double-booking.

4. You Receive Instant Notifications

Get notifications via email or SMS with full call summary and customer details.

View call logs, transcripts, recordings, and notes. Perfect for tracking leads and staff performance.

The AI can be trained for your industry: dealerships, dental clinics, salons, real estate, home services, restaurants, and more.
